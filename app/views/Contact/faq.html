For Applicants

How anonymous am I?

We keep Apache access logs, and we'll call the cops if you do anything really screwed up. But, we don't post your IP address or email with the question. We don't do anything to prevent you from revealing personal details, so a lot of the privacy is up to you.

How do I know it's not an HR employee answering all the questions?

You don't. If you can't figure it out from the content of the question you should write a new question. Usually, it's pretty easy to tell someone who is in your industry.

How private is my email?

We don't publish your email and we don't sell it for spam. It's only used to send you updates.

What if I lose the link to the question?

Sorry, it's gone. If you supplied an email address, we'll email you when a new answer is posted. If not, you're out of luck.

For Employers

How do I keep my employees from posting negative answers?

You can't. This is a tool to allow open, unfettered communication. You have to take the good with the bad.

However, you can delete questions. If the answers get out of hand, you can always delete the whole question. This will be obvious to the applicant. If the applicant supplied an email, the offending answers will be emailed before you can delete.

A basic rule of thumb; if you expect to get negative answers, don't use this service. And, if you're a generally good employer or manager, you should be ready to discuss issues openly. No company is perfect, but the good ones own up to their difficulties.

Can't people use this for social engineering attacks?

Yep. I understand your problem, and I'm glad that I work for a smaller company where we can expect the team to not be so gullible. I don't know what to tell you expect that maybe ApplicantChatter isn't for you.

Are my questions private?

Only employees of your company can see a list of all the questions. Anyone with the link to the question can see it. Having the link to a question doesn't give you access to any other question.

Can anyone ask me a question?

Anyone with the link to the "ask a question" page can ask as many questions as they want. Also, people will be able to find you if you go to the Settings section and check "Publish yourself on the Ask Page?"
