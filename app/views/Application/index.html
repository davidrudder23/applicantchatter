ApplicantChatter is a website where job seekers can ask your current employees questions anonymously.

Companies Love Us!

Go beyond promises in job posts. Prove to potential employees that you have a great place to work!

By opening your employees to applicants, you're letting the applicants know that your advertisements are true. By allowing employees to talk with applicants, both sides learn better if there is a good fit.

Job Seekers Rely on Us!

Better employers use ApplicantChatter because they're the ones confident enough to let applicants and employees communicate.

ApplicantChatter allows you, the job seeker, to ask questions directly to the team you will be hired into. You can know what the environment and team is like before you apply. Since the service is anonymous, and for both the applicant and the employee, you can trust the question and answer to be 100% confidential.
