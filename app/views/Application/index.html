Attract better candidates and ensure a better team fit!

ApplicantChatter is an addition to your recruitment process.

When you signup, you get a link where applicants can post questions to your employees. You also get a management screen where you can moderate the questions and answers.

With ApplicantChatter, you get higher quality, better qualified applicants. The applicants will come into the process with fewer questions. Your team will have a better fit on day one.

Companies Love Us!

Go beyond promises in job posts. Prove to potential employees that you have a great place to work!

By opening your employees to applicants, you're letting the applicants know that your advertisements are true. By allowing employees to talk with applicants, both sides learn better if there is a good fit.

Job Seekers Rely on Us!

Better employers use ApplicantChatter because they're the ones confident enough to let applicants and employees communicate.

ApplicantChatter allows you, the job seeker, to ask questions directly to the team you will be hired into. You can know what the environment and team is like before you apply. Since the service is anonymous, for both the applicant and the employee, you can trust the question and answer to be 100% confidential.
